# 💰 Budget Vision

> Your monthly financial snapshot — powered by smart insights and live charts.

A premium budget calculator with live donut charts, category breakdown, and AI-style financial insights. Built for the Indian market with ₹ Rupee support.

![HTML](https://img.shields.io/badge/built%20with-HTML%2FCSS%2FJS-blue) ![Chart.js](https://img.shields.io/badge/charts-Chart.js-orange) ![No API](https://img.shields.io/badge/API-none-green)

---

## ✨ Features

- **Live donut chart** — updates as you type, shows spending distribution
- **8 expense categories** — Housing, Food, Transport, Health, Entertainment, Shopping, Education, Other
- **4 metric cards** — Income, Total Spent, Remaining, Savings Rate (color-coded)
- **Category progress bars** — shows each expense as % of income
- **Smart insights** — personalized feedback based on your numbers
- **50-30-20 Rule Analyzer** — compares your spending to the ideal split
- **₹ Indian Rupee support** — built for the Indian market
- **Works 100% offline** — no server, no API, no data leaves your browser

## 🧠 The 50-30-20 Rule

The app analyzes your spending against this proven rule:
- **50%** → Needs (rent, food, transport, health, education)
- **30%** → Wants (entertainment, shopping, other)
- **20%** → Savings
